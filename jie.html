Jie Xiong


Associate Professor

College of Information and Computer Sciences

University of Massachusetts Amherst

Email: jxiong@cs.umass.edu

I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science, University College London in 2015 (supported by Google European Doctoral Fellowship). My research is supported by NSF Career Award and NIH R01.

I have broad interests in wireless sensing, smart IoT, cyber-physical systems, mobile computing and wireless networking.
